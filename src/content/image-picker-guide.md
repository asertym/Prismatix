# Image Picker Guide

Quickly extract color palettes from any image.

## Getting Started

- **Upload**: Select a file from your computer or paste an image directly from your clipboard.
- **Analyze**: The tool automatically generates a palette of dominant colors and their proportions.
- **Semantic Swatches**: See colors categorized into six semantic roles, complete with text color recommendations for accessibility.
- **Manual Sampling**: Click any part of the image to pick a specific color.

## Tips

- **Hint**: Click any sampled color or swatch to copy its value to your clipboard.
- **Hint**: Your manual picks are saved in the picker history for easy access.
